Going into 2016 the Cubs were clicking on all cylinders. The Cubs opened the season with a 9-0 win over The Angels and never looked back. By the time the post season rolled around the Cubs were cruising. In the World Series however, the Cubs found themselves down 3-1. The Cubs managed to rally at home to force game six, then game seven. Finally, on November 2nd, the Cubs battled their way through game seven, pulling ahead, then ultimately prevailing in extra innings. The Chicago Cubs had won the World Series.
