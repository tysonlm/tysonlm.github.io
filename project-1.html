K With the rise of the accessability of media on screen, people read information in print less and less. due to the popularity of ebooks and online news sites, designers are putting more time into creating typefaces that are ideal for reading on screen. Sol Matas designed Bitter with on screen readability in mind. Bitter is featured on more than 1.2 million websites. (Matas)
